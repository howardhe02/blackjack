Features

1) The player may hit as many times as they want, as long as their sum does not go over 21
2) The dealer will draw again after the player decides to hold if the dealer's sum is less than 16

Additional Features

1) The dealer is Yumeko Jabami from the Kakegurui show
2) Yumeko Jabami tells the player important game information, such as if the player won, loss, bet restriction and many more. Yumeko Jabami will say anything that should be said. She's the narrator and also the person you face
3) Everytime Yumeko Jabami says something, the box will flash to alert the player to look at what she has to say
3) There is a music box in game that contains many functions
4) The player may choose their avatar and enter a name
5) The player never truly wins
6) The dealer never truly loses
7) There are custom achievements
