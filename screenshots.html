Screenshots

This is the game table. Most GUI components are in this screen and the main game takes place using most of these components.
This is the side bar to the main game page. Most player actions are displayed here
The GUI component on the left is the music box. It displays what music is playing and what titles are avaliable to be played. The GUI component on the right is the custom player profile and balance.
The box in the center is the pot. This is where the player bet is displayed. It is updated when raised or a new round starts. The girl in the top right is Yumeko Jabami from Kakegurui and acts as the dealer as well as helps the player stay informed on the current game.
This is the starting page before playing Blackjack. This page shows selectable characters. On this page, the player may also choose a name. If left blank, the name tag on the player profile displays DefaultMcDefaultFace.
