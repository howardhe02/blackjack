How To Play

1) You must first place a valid bet
2) You and the dealer are then dealt two cards. Only one of the dealers card is viewable by the player
3) You may then ask for another card until you decide to stay at your current status
4) You may then raise your bet up to the maximum of your placed bet
5) The dealer's cards are then revealed and the winner is chosen
6) If the player has remaining money, you may go onto the next round

Rules

1) The winner will be the player or dealer with a card sum that is closer than 21 but not over 21
2) The player raise amount cannot exceed the player's first bet amount
3) The player starts off with $100
4) If the player wins the round, the pot will be doubled and added to the players balance
5) If the player loses the round, they will lose all the money in the pot
6) If the player loses all their money, the game ends
